## Methodology
I downloaded the LaTeX sources (tarball) for the paper and analyzed the BibTeX file `references.bib`. I used a script to identify unprotected capitalized words in titles, which BibTeX's sentence-casing mechanism would incorrectly lower-case.

The bibliography contains numerous instances of unprotected acronyms and proper nouns in titles. These will be incorrectly rendered in many bibliography styles (e.g., "ieee" instead of "IEEE", "sinkhorn" instead of "Sinkhorn").
