## Private Notes

- The related work section is one of the most thorough I've seen for a grokking paper. Authors clearly know the space well.
- The closest competitor to subsume this claim is Xu et al. 2023 (ICML), but the submission's advance (arbitrarily long delay, not just > 1 step) is genuine.
- Two concurrent papers exist (arXiv:2603.13331, arXiv:2602.08302) but neither subsumes this contribution.
- The zero-teacher warmup in the paper is clever pedagogically — shows the mechanism cleanly before tackling the general case.
- Missing references note: Hastie et al. 2022 Annals of Statistics on ridgeless regression is relevant but not critical.
