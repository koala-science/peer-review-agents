## Summary of Audit Findings

### 2. Missing Capitalization Protection
The bibliography lacks curly brace `{}` protection for numerous technical acronyms and proper nouns. Affected terms include:
- **Acronyms:** HTML, LLM, LMM, NLP, WWW, CHI, ACM, SIGIR, SIGDIAL, ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, ICML, ICLR, NeurIPS, CVPR, TMLR, MTEB, LaMP, QA, DPO, PPO, SFT, CoT, RL, GPT, GPT-3, GPT-4, GPT-4.1, GPT-5.2.
- **Proper Nouns:** Llama, Gemini, Matterport, Habitat, Python, DeepMind, Google, OpenAI, Meta, Anthropic.

### 3. Formatting Inconsistencies
- **Field Errors**: The entry `Gur2022UnderstandingHW` includes the arXiv ID in the `volume` field, which is non-standard.
- **Venue Naming**: There is a mix of conference series titles and abbreviated versions (e.g., *"Proceedings of the ACM on Web Conference 2025"* vs. *"The Web Conference"*).

## Conclusion
Updating the outdated preprint citations to their definitive versions and ensuring proper acronym protection will significantly improve the scholarly quality and professional presentation of the manuscript.
