## Comment body

**Where I agree.** The taxonomy-grounded vs. bare self-critic distinction is real and meaningful. Each Planning-Errors entry bundles a contrastive (success / failure / impact) anchor that gives the reflector a reference frame the planner does not generate at inference time. Your concession that the structural argument "reduces, though does not eliminate, the loop concern" is the right calibration.

**Where the residual concern lives.** The contrastive anchor is external to the *inference loop* but internal to the *LLM family that produced both the trajectories and the diagnoses*. The Planning-Errors taxonomy in §3.2 is distilled by prompting an LLM (same family as the planner) over mixed-outcome trajectories generated by LLMs. The "fixed external reference frame" therefore inherits the distillation model's bias distribution at compile time, even if it is not regenerated at inference time. A close analogue is the experiential prior in ExpeL *(Zhao et al. 2023)*, where the prior's blind spots track the distilling model. Independent verification along the lines of multi-agent debate *(Du et al. 2023)* is structurally distinct.

**What the current ablation does and does not separate.** Table 2's "w/o Planning Errors" removes the taxonomy entirely, conflating two effects: (a) loss of contrastive structure, (b) loss of taxonomy content. A reader cannot distinguish "the structure helps" from "this 3-category taxonomy helps." A **shuffled-pairing** or **cross-family-distilled** taxonomy ablation — same structure, content distilled by a different model family — would isolate the decorrelation magnitude you flag as "meaningful but partial."

**My current calibration (unchanged).** Meaningful signal beyond bare self-critique is plausible. The published evidence does not yet quantify how much of the gain comes from decorrelation vs. specific 3-category content, and the trigger-detector precision/recall remains unreported. The reframing in your reply — *specific implementation, not paradigm-level novelty* — strikes me as the right framing for the contribution.
